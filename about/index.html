About us · La Puente, CA · Los Angeles County

Leak Specialists Built for the Hub City

La Puente Leak Repair Experts exists to do one thing well in one place: find and fix water leaks in the housing stock of La Puente and its San Gabriel Valley neighbors. Call (626) 898-6169 any hour and you reach that focus directly.

Why a leak company for this city in particular

La Puente takes its name from a bridge the Portolá expedition built across San Jose Creek in 1769, and the land under the city spent a century as Rancho La Puente, the vast 1840s grant worked by William Workman and John Rowland. The walnut groves gave way to tract homes after the war, and on August 1, 1956 the city incorporated in the middle of that boom. Most of the houses standing today were plumbed within a decade of that date.

That history is not decoration. It is the service map. Pre-war blocks around Old Town and Downtown still carry galvanized supply and cast iron drains. The huge 1950s and 1960s cohort runs original copper that has now spent sixty-plus years in hard Main San Gabriel Basin groundwater, roughly 10 to 17 grains per gallon. Later infill near Glenford Park and the Bassett border mixes mid-life copper with PEX. Each era fails in its own way, and knowing which failure belongs to which street is most of the job.

How we operate

Detection before demolition. Every job starts with acoustic listening, thermal imaging, pressure testing, or tracer gas as needed, so the leak is pinpointed before anything is cut. You see the evidence, then you get options: spot repair, reroute, or repipe, each with upfront pricing and a free on-site estimate. Permits are pulled whenever the scope requires them, and the work is performed by plumbers licensed in California through the Contractors State License Board.

We keep the promises small and keep all of them. The phone at (626) 898-6169 is answered around the clock. Pricing is stated before work begins. And if a repair is not worth making, we say so.
