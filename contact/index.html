While you wait, if water is actively flowing

Find your main shutoff valve. In most La Puente homes it sits on the riser where the water line enters the house, often near a front hose bib, with a second angle stop at the meter box by the sidewalk. Turn it clockwise until it stops. Shutting the main protects your flooring and drywall and costs you nothing while help is on the way. Unsure where it is?
